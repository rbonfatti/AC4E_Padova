## Prompt

```text
Use examples/card-krueger as the running example and examples/starter_article as
the project skeleton. Do not edit raw data. Draft a project brief with research
question, data source, method, expected outputs, confidentiality boundaries, and
out-of-scope items. Show the diff or save it to docs/project_brief.md.
```

## Verification

The draft must state:

- treatment group;
- comparison group;
- timing;
- outcome;
- unit of observation;
- sample restriction;
- synthetic-data caveat;
- no substantive causal claim from the teaching CSV.
